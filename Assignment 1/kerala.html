Kerala

Kerala, a state on India's tropical Malabar Coast, has nearly 600km of Arabian Sea shoreline. It's known for its palm-lined beaches and backwaters, a network of canals.

  • Districts - 14
  • Capital City - Thiruvanandapuram
  • Largest City - Thiruvanandapuram
  • Governor - Hansraj Bhardwaj
  • Chief Minister -Oommen Chandy
  • Population - 33,387,677
  • Tourist spots - Alleppey (Alappuzha), Wayanad, Cochin ,Munnar, Kumarakom, Kovalam etc..,
